Lord Sri Rama is the seventh avatar of Vishnu, known for his righteousness and virtue.

Spiritual Significance

The epic Ramayana narrates his life, emphasizing dharma, devotion, and moral excellence. Rama's virtue inspires millions of devotees worldwide.

What is Rama Koti?

Rama Koti is the devotional practice of chanting or writing the name of Lord Sri Rama one crore (10 million) times. This sacred act promotes concentration, inner peace, and connection to divine blessings.

Traditionally, it is done by hand, but now also embraced digitally, making this powerful spiritual exercise accessible anytime and anywhere.

"Repetition of Rama's name is believed to open the door to divine grace and eternal bliss."

"To perform Rama Koti, simply click the 'Insert 1 Crore Rama Koti' button and 1 crore Ramakoti entries will be digitally inserted and stored on your mobile device."
